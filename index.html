About Me

I blame Mr. Rogers for my insatiable need to know how everything is made. And so here I am today, fully capable at building web pages and apps, because I just had to know how they were made. I love it so much that I decided to change careers be a full time developer.


I am a self-taught developers which has given me a unique perspective when listening to a client or approaching a problem. I understand what it is like to be a consumer and an employee on the operational side of business. Mostly, I understand that letting go of an ego and listening to listen is key to creating amazing solutions and avoid unnecessary re-work. Turns out our mothers were right, if you listen the first times things will be easier.
